# Helical Archive Author Map

This map records the public GitHub/repo footprint I could verify for the
authors and groups named in the archived M3D-C1 helical-coil meeting notes.
It does **not** claim to identify a public mirror of the helical `C1.h5`
payload. It only records what is publicly visible and relevant.

## Source Context

The helical benchmark appears in the archived PPPL meeting minutes as:

- [12_20_21.pdf](https://m3dc1.pppl.gov/Weekly/12_20_21.pdf)
- [12_06_21.pdf](https://m3dc1.pppl.gov/Weekly/12_06_21.pdf)

## Conclusion

I could not verify a public repository that contains the helical benchmark
`C1.h5` files. The public repository trail points to code, readers, and
adjacent fusion tooling, but the benchmark output itself appears to remain on
internal PPPL storage.
